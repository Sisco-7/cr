Our Facilities

Winsome Model Schools is equipped with modern facilities to support a well-rounded education. Our infrastructure is designed to enhance learning, creativity, and physical development.

Classrooms

State-of-the-art classrooms with interactive smartboards, ergonomic furniture, and a conducive learning environment.

Science Laboratories

Advanced labs for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology, fully equipped for hands-on experiments and research.

Library

A well-stocked library with over 10,000 books, e-books, journals, and a quiet study area.

Sports Facilities

World-class sports facilities including a football field, basketball court, swimming pool, and gymnasium.

ICT Center

Modern computer labs with high-speed internet, coding software, and digital learning resources.

School Clinic

A fully equipped clinic with qualified nurses to provide first-aid and emergency care.
