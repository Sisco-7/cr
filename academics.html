Academics at Winsome Model Schools

At Winsome Model Schools, our academic programs are designed to meet the highest standards of education, aligning with WAEC and NECO requirements while fostering critical thinking, creativity, and a love for learning. We offer a comprehensive curriculum that prepares students for both national and international success.

Curriculum Overview

Our curriculum covers a wide range of subjects, including:

  • Mathematics
  • English Language and Literature
  • Sciences (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • Social Studies and Civic Education
  • Arts and Humanities
  • Vocational Studies (e.g., ICT, Home Economics)

We also offer specialized programs for gifted students and remedial support for those who need it.

Teaching Methodology

We employ a student-centered approach, integrating modern technology with traditional teaching methods. Our classrooms feature interactive smartboards, and we use project-based learning, group discussions, and hands-on experiments to engage students. Our teachers are trained in differentiated instruction to cater to diverse learning styles.

Academic Achievements

Our students consistently achieve a 98% pass rate in WAEC and NECO exams. Highlights include:

  • Winner of the 2024 National Mathematics Olympiad
  • First Place in the Nigerian Science Fair (2023)
  • Top 10 in the National Debate Championship (2022)

We also celebrate 100% university admission rates for our graduates.
