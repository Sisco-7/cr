Admissions

Joining Winsome Model Schools is the first step towards a bright future for your child. Our admissions process is designed to be transparent and straightforward, ensuring every student has the opportunity to thrive.

Admission Process

  1. Purchase and complete the application form (available online or at the school office).
  2. Submit the form with required documents.
  3. Attend the entrance examination on the scheduled date.
  4. Participate in an interview with the admissions panel.
  5. Receive admission offer and complete registration.

Requirements

  • Birth Certificate
  • Previous School Records (if applicable)
  • Two Passport Photographs
  • Medical Report
  • Proof of Payment for Application Fee

Fees Structure

Our fees vary by academic level:

  • Junior Secondary: ₦500,000 per term
  • Senior Secondary: ₦600,000 per term

Additional fees may apply for boarding, uniforms, and extracurricular activities. Scholarships and financial aid are available for qualifying students.
