About Winsome Model Schools

Founded in 1995, Winsome Model Schools has established itself as a cornerstone of educational excellence in Ifako, Lagos, Nigeria. As a fully registered institution with the West African Examinations Council (WAEC) and the National Examinations Council (NECO), we are dedicated to providing a transformative education that prepares students for success in a globalized world.

Our journey began with a vision to create a nurturing environment where young minds can thrive. Over the past three decades, we have grown into a premier educational institution, known for our rigorous academic programs, state-of-the-art facilities, and commitment to holistic development. Our graduates have gone on to excel in various fields, from medicine and engineering to the arts and entrepreneurship, both in Nigeria and internationally.

At Winsome Model Schools, we believe education is more than just academics—it’s about building character, fostering creativity, and instilling a sense of responsibility. Our core values of integrity, innovation, and community service guide everything we do, ensuring that every student leaves our halls ready to make a positive impact on the world.

Our Vision

To nurture innovative leaders equipped to thrive in a global society by providing a world-class education that fosters critical thinking, creativity, and ethical values.

Our Mission

To deliver a transformative learning experience through quality teaching, modern facilities, and a holistic approach that balances academic excellence with personal growth.
